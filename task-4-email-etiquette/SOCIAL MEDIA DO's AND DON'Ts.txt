SOCIAL MEDIA DO's AND DON'Ts

DO's:
- Think before posting anything online
- Keep your profile professional and clean
- Use privacy settings to protect your information
- Respect others and communicate politely
- Verify information before sharing

DON'Ts:
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information
- Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content
- Don’t spread fake news or unverified information
- Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling
- Don’t ignore your digital footprint
